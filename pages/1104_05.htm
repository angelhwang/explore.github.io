Two communities of magnetic organisms begin an evolutionary journey through wild and colorful lands.
Film commissioned by Animario, an Animation Festival based in Madrid dedicated to contemporary animation.
Directed and animated by Dvein.
Sound Design by Banjo Soundscapes,
Type design by Alex Trochut.
In this film, we wanted to play and experiment, feel the materials and animation, like when we were kids and played in the backyard. The decision on the technique to be used was crucial. Take a look at the "Behind the Scenes" video to see how fun the process was.
